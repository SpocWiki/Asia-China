## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The **Taklamakan** Desert ( TAK-lə-mə-KAN) is a desert in northwest China. 
> Located inside the Tarim Basin in Southern Xinjiang, it is bounded by 
> - the Kunlun Mountains to the south, 
> - the Pamir Mountains to the west, 
> - the Tian Shan range to the north, and 
> - the Gobi Desert to the east.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taklamakan%20Desert)

# [[Temple_of_Confucius,Qufu]] 

#is_/same_as :: [[WD~Temple_of_Confucius,_Qufu,339822]] 

## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Temple of Confucius (Chinese: 孔廟; pinyin: Kǒng miào) in Qufu, Shandong Province, 
> is the largest and most renowned temple of Confucius in East Asia.
>
> Since 1994, the Temple of Confucius has been  part of the UNESCO World Heritage Site 
> "Temple and Cemetery of Confucius and the Kong Family Mansion in Qufu". 
>
> The two other parts of the site are the nearby Kong Family Mansion, 
> where the main-line descendants of Confucius lived, 
> and the Cemetery of Confucius a few kilometers to the north, 
> where Confucius and many of his descendants have been buried. 
> 
> Those three sites are collectively known in Qufu as San Kong (三孔), 
> i.e. "The Three Confucian [sites]".
>
> There is a 72-meter-tall statue of Confucius made of brass and reinforced with steel. 
> Qufu, Shandong province, 
> is the birthplace of the ancient Chinese educator and philosopher.
>
